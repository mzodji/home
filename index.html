üîé Research

My doctoral research focuses on free boundary problems for models describing the flow of fluid mixtures. The constituents are assumed to be immiscible (for instance, gas bubbles in a liquid) and are separated by one or more interfaces of zero thickness. The objective is to investigate the persistance of interface regularity over time; this involves, in particular, establishing well-posedness of the underlying models. I employ Hoff-type energy estimates together with Lagrangian changes of variables and harmonic analysis tools.

Currently, I study a fluid‚Äìstructure interaction model, and the regularity of Lagrangian trajectories for solutions of the incompressible Euler equations. Both projects draw on Littlewood‚ÄìPaley theory and microlocal analysis.
